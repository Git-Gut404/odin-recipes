Bell Peppers and Beef

A delicious food like substance for the perfect steakout

Drop whatever you're doing, this new recipe will blow your tongue straight to Mars. It's the perfect steak for both work and play. Draw bounties to yourself with the delicious smell of the all new BELL PEPPERS AND BEEF recipe. An easy to make taste experience, that will make your mama proud.

Serves 4

Quest prerequisites:

  • 4 Bell Peppers
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 lb Lean ground beef
  • 1 tsp Butter
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Minced cloves of garlic
  • beef bouillon cube
  • Soy and Teriyaki Sauce
  • 1 cup shiitake mushrooms
  • Boiling water
  • Seasoning
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Paprika

Steps for the culinarily challenged

  1. Chop onion and bell peppers into thin strips
  2. Chop shiitake mushrooms into quarters
  3. Peel the garlic and cut off the cap
  4. Pour the olive oil into a pan
  5. Stir-fry onion, bell pepper, mushrooms and garlic with the butter for about 5 minutes or less
  6. Take bouillon cube and crumble over the mixture, along with the soy and teriyaki sauce
  7. Season with salt, pepper, paprika and boiling water and stir until well blended
  8. Place mixture in a bowl and serve it
